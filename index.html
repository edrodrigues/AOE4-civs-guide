Age of Empires IV Civilizations Guide

Abbasid Dynasty

Strengths

  • Strong economic bonuses
  • Access to unique technologies
  • Powerful late-game units

Weaknesses

  • Weak early-game defense
  • Reliance on gold for unique units

Special Units

  • Camel Archer
  • Mameluke

Best Strategies

  • Focus on economic development
  • Use Camel Archers for harassment
  • Save gold for Mamelukes in the late game
